### 🔋 TRiNNOVATION OWL · DC-Showroom Digitalization

**TH OWL / iFE Institut für Energieforschung · Aug 2025 – Feb 2026**

Led the digitalization of the DC-Transferzentrum at TH OWL within the federally funded **TRiNNOVATION OWL** Impulsprojekt. Transformed a static power-electronics demonstration lab into a connected, monitored, remotely accessible IoT environment.

### ⚡ Smart EV Charger Monitoring + Control

**iFE / TH OWL**

Industrial IoT integration connecting a **Beckhoff IPC C6920-0060** (TwinCAT 3) with an **ambiCHARGE 11 kW EV charger** over MQTT. Two C# desktop applications (AmbiMonitor + AmbiBroadcaster), Mosquitto broker, simulated test environment.

**Hashim Medical City, Hyderabad · 2019 – 2020**

End-to-end embedded hospital call system, designed and deployed for operational use at HMC. Led a 3-person team owning the full product lifecycle from circuit design to field deployment. ATmega328, Embedded C, LabVIEW, RS485, multilayer PCB, SolidWorks acrylic enclosure.
